Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2026

Overview

Yusuf's Personal AI Assistant is a personal application designed for individual use. This privacy policy explains how the application handles your data.

Data Access

This application accesses your Gmail and Google Drive data only with your explicit permission through Google OAuth. All data access is performed:

  • On your behalf and only when you request actions
  • Using secure OAuth tokens that you control
  • In accordance with Google's API terms of service

Data Usage

Your data is used solely to provide the AI assistant functionality you request, such as:

  • Reading and searching your emails
  • Drafting and sending email responses
  • Accessing and editing files in your Google Drive
  • Creating and modifying Google Docs

We do not share, sell, or use your data for any other purpose.

Data Storage

OAuth tokens are stored securely and are only used to access your Google services as requested. No email content or file data is stored permanently by this application.

Third-Party Services

This application uses:

  • Google APIs - For accessing Gmail, Drive, and Docs. Your use of these services is subject to Google's privacy policy.
  • OpenAI/Anthropic APIs - For AI processing. Only the content you explicitly send to the assistant is processed.

Your Rights

You can revoke access to your Google account at any time by:

  • Going to Google Account Permissions
  • Removing access for "Yusuf Personal AI"
